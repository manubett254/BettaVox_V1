About BettaVox

BettaVox is an AI-powered system that analyzes voice characteristics to predict age and gender.

Using advanced machine learning techniques, BettaVox extracts features from voice samples and provides accurate predictions based on extensive training data.

How It Works

  • Upload or record your voice sample.
  • BettaVox extracts key features from the audio.
  • Our AI model predicts your age group and gender.
